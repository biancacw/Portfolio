My Origin

I am currently a junior at UNC-Chapel Hill, but I am originally from Charlotte, NC. Since I was a child I have spent my summers in Brazilian rainforest surrounded by wildlife and nature, which inspired me to pursue a career in the environmental sector.

My Goals
